Our Journey

How VoyageX grew from an idea to a community.

Every milestone taught us something new about what travelers really want—authenticity, simplicity, and moments that last.

2018
The Idea
Lila Marquez quit her travel journalism job to start a small travel blog focused on hidden gems. She quickly realized travelers wanted more than just recommendations—they wanted someone to handle the details.
2019
Launch
VoyageX officially launched with 3 curated routes in Bali, Iceland, and Kyoto. The first 100 trips sold out in 2 weeks.
2021
Expansion
We added 12 new destinations and launched our 24/7 concierge service. Our community grew to 1,000+ travelers.
2023
Sustainability Focus
We launched our Responsible Travel Initiative, committing to 100% eco-friendly stays and 5% of profits to local community projects.
Today
Community
We now have 128 curated routes, 3,000+ happy travelers, and a team of 24 experts dedicated to making every trip perfect.
