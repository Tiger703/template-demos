About

A studio of makers, thinkers, and storytellers.

For over a decade we've helped brands across fashion, tech, hospitality, and the arts find their voice and tell their story.

Our Story

From a small Brooklyn apartment to a global studio.

Vesper was founded in 2014 by three friends who met in design school. We started with a simple belief: brands deserve more than templates and trends.

Today, we're a team of 26 designers, developers, writers, and strategists working from studios in Brooklyn, London, and Tokyo.

Our Values

What guides everything we do.

🎯

Craft First

We'd rather ship one extraordinary thing than ten ordinary ones. Quality over quantity, always.

💬

Honest Work

We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want. Always with kindness and respect.

🤝

Long Game

Most clients come back. We build for lasting relationships, not quick transactions.

Origins

How three friends started a global studio.

Alex, Maya, and James met at the Rhode Island School of Design in 2010. Different paths brought them together: Alex in branding, Maya in editorial, James in engineering.

By 2014, they'd had enough of agency work. They quit their jobs, rented a tiny Brooklyn apartment, and opened the studio with $5,000 and a single client.
