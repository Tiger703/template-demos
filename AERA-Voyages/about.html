The People Behind Your Perfect Journey

AERA was born from a simple belief: travel should feel personal, intentional, and beautifully designed — not mass-produced.

Our story

We started as travelers who couldn't find what we were looking for.

In 2012, three friends took a trip to Morocco. The hotel was fine. The food was fine. The tours were fine. But nothing felt personal. Nothing felt designed for them. That trip planted a seed.

They asked: what if every journey felt like a magazine spread come to life? What if the planning process itself felt exciting? What if you had a real human who knew every hidden alley, every quiet café, every sunset viewpoint — and put it all together just for you?

That question became AERA.

"Tourism shouldn't feel like tourism. It should feel like a story being written — with you as the main character."

— Elena Reyes, Co-founder

Today, we operate across 84 countries with a team of 45 curators, local guides, and travel designers. We've planned over 8,500 journeys. And the philosophy hasn't changed: every trip should feel like it was made for one person — because it was.

Our Promise

Every journey is personally designed, never templated. Your trip exists only for you.
