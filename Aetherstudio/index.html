Maison Inquiries

Frequently asked questions

What is your typical commission timeline?+

Boutique digital portals typically require 8-12 weeks of meticulous craftsmanship. Full-scale physical architectural commissions and enterprise digital ecosystems range from 6 to 18 months depending on spatial complexity.

How does AETHER handle client confidentiality?+

We operate under military-grade NDAs and maintain strict privacy protocols. Our patron list is never disclosed publicly without explicit written consent. All communications are routed through secure encrypted channels.

Do you collaborate with external engineering or construction firms?+

Yes. While we provide complete turnkey design and digital engineering, we frequently liaise with your retained local construction developers and family office legal teams to ensure flawless physical realization.

What digital technologies does the Maison employ?+

We build upon cutting-edge enterprise stacks: React, Next.js, Three.js/WebGL for immersive 3D motion, GSAP, and robust headless CMS platforms (Sanity, Strapi) backed by highly secure AWS/Vercel cloud infrastructure.

What is included in your perpetual concierge care?+

Our concierge service guarantees 24/7 priority engineering support, instant security patching, continuous performance optimization, content updates, and annual design refreshments to ensure absolute supremacy.
