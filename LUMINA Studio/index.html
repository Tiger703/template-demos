About Lumina

A studio of creative sunshine.

We are designers, developers, and dreamers crafting bright digital experiences from sunny offices around the world.

Our Story

From passion to studio.

Lumina was born in a sun-drenched apartment in 2010 with two laptops, a coffee maker, and three friends who believed digital design could feel as warm as morning light.

Today we work with bold brands across the globe, blending warmth with technology to create experiences that linger.
